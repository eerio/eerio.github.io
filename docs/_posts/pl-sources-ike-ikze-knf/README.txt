the *.xls files in this directory are backups of files
found on KNF (Polish SEC) website using their
search tool here:
https://www.knf.gov.pl/publikacje_i_opracowania/dane_statystyczne?dateTo=&searchText=IKE&categories=/publikacje_i_opracowania/dane_statystyczne/emerytalny&dateFrom=

these are lists of IKE and IKZE providers in Poland published semi-annually from June 2017
to June 2024 (December 2024 will be published on December 31st)

it gives insight into the history of IKE/IKZE accounts in Poland and also
guarantees that you know about every provider in existence, because
they don't always come up in web search!
